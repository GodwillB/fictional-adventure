Additional Information

In particular, Afriland First Bank South Sudan PLC is a full-service Bank in existence since 2011. It received an operations license from Bank of South Sudan on the 24th of May 2012 and currently maintains a Share Capital of USD 15,000,000 and Total Asset Base of USD 35,000,000.

Additionally, AFBSS operates one branch at its head office located at Plot No.12, Block A, Hai Cinema Road, P.O. Box 640 Juba, South Sudan.

AFBSS has demonstrated resilience and is adequately capitalised in the face of considerable stress attributable to the economic impact of general increase in prices of goods and services (inflation). The inflation has mainly affected our profits through the exchange loss registered of SSP 358 million in June, 2022

The profits for the mid-year 2022 have increased by 79% from SSP 119 million recorded in 2021 to SSP 213 million recorded in June, 2022. The total deposits for the mid-year 2022 have increased by 210% from SSP 6.1 billion recorded in 2021 to SSP 14.8 billion recorded in June, 2 .

The total assets for the mid-year 2022 have increased by 166% from SSP 9.4 billion recorded in 2021 to SSP 18.9 billion recorded in June, 2022.
